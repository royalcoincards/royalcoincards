"We saw a gap in the market — and we filled it with gold."

Royal Coin Cards was born out of a simple observation: Pakistan's jewellers and bullion dealers deserved world-class coin packaging — but no one was making it here. So we built it ourselves.

Based in Karachi, we are the first and only dedicated wholesale coin card manufacturer in Pakistan. Our team of skilled craftspeople handcrafts each card with care — because the packaging of gold and silver should reflect its value.
